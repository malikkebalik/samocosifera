Jung Eun-ji is a South Korean singer, songwriter, actress, and voiceover artist who is best known for having been a member of the famous South Korean girl group, Apink. As a musician, she has worked on a number of albums, EPs, and songs such as The Space, Dream, Une Année, Pink Memory, Pink Doll, Seven Springs of Apink, and One & Six.

Jung Eun-ji Facts

  • Growing up, she wanted to become a vocal trainer.
  • Artists like Park Cho-rong, Yoon Bo-mi, Son Na-eun, Kim Nam-joo, Oh Ha-young, and Hong Yoo-kyung have also been a part of Apink.
  • As a member of Apink, Jung Eun-ji released her debut album, Une Année, on May 9, 2012. The album comprised of songs such as April 19th, Hush, I Got You, and Sky High and was ranked at number #4 in the Gaon album chart.
  • From 2012 to 2013, she portrayed the iconic character of Elle Woods in the theatre production of the 2007 musical, Legally Blonde.
  • Jung Eun-ji has a habit of singing almost all the time.
  • In 2012, she played the role of Sung Shi-won, a devoted fangirl of boyband H.O.T., in the romantic teen comedy-drama TV show, Reply 1997, in which she starred alongside the likes of Seo In-guk, Eun Ji-won, and Lee Si-eon. For this role, Jung Eun-ji won a number of awards such as the 1st K-Drama Star Award in the “Rising Star” category and 49th Baeksang Arts Award in the “Best New TV Actress” category.
  • She has also appeared on many other shows like That Winter, The Wind Blows, Lovers of Music, Untouchable, Cheer Up!, etc.
  • As a voiceover artist, she has provided vocals to the character of Carrie in My Pet Ozzy and Shiny in Saving Santa.
  • Jung Eun-ji has been friends with Rainbow‘s Go Woo-ri.
