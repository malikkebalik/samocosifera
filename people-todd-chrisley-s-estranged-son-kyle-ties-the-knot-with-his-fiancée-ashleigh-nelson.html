People: Todd Chrisley's Estranged Son Kyle Ties the Knot with His Fiance Ashleigh Nelson

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  394 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Todd Chrisley's estranged son Kyle Chrisley has tied the knot with Ashleigh Nelson, who revealed she knew from the very beginning that they would marry.

Kyle Chrisley and Ashleigh Nelson are officially wed. According to a marriage certificate that People obtained, the couple tied the knot on March 30 in St. Augustine, Florida.

The certificate further revealed that Kyle and Nelson got the license in St. Johns County a day before their wedding. There were no witnesses listed on the document.

Todd Chrisley at the 'Chrisley Knows Best' panel at the 2016 NBCUniversal Summer Press Day at Four Seasons Hotel Westlake Village on April 1, 2016 in Westlake Village, California | Photo: Getty Images

In a chat with People, Nelson said she always knew from the start that she would tie the knot with Kyle. She also said that to know Kyle's family, the Chrisleys, is to love them, and her partner is no different.

Nelson also added that Kyle is hopeful that he can be half the husband that his dad, Todd Chrisley, is to Julie Chrisley. Meanwhile, Todd and Julie are yet to speak publicly about Kyle's marriage.

In February, Kyle announced that he was engaged to Nelson. He shared a picture of her smiling as she flashed her engagement ring.

In the post's caption, Kyle wrote, "I couldn’t imagine my life without you, and now I don’t have to." He expressed his love to Nelson, adding that he was happy she said yes.

The father and son expressed their desire to see each other before expressing love for each other.

Nelson also shared the picture Kyle posted on her Instagram page. In her caption, she said she kept her life away from the public because she is always worried about what people may think.

Nelson further said that such things did not matter anymore before announcing that she and Kyle were getting married. Many of her followers congratulated her.

A clip from the March 25 episode of the all-new "Chrisley Knows Best" showed as Todd received a surprise phone call from Kyle. In the emotional clip, the father and son expressed their desire to see each other before expressing love for each other.

Congratulations are in order for Kyle and Nelson, and the Chrisley family and fans will be hoping that this new chapter leads to even more happiness for Kyle.
