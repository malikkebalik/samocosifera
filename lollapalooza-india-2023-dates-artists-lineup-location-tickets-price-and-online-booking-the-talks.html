Lollapalooza India Date And Venue

Lollapalooza India will be held from the 28th to the 29th of January in Mumbai, Maharashtra at Mahalaxmi Race Course. Imagine Dragons and The Strokes will headline the festival and Jackson Wang of GOT7 to bring Magicman to Mumbai.

Rock bands Imagine Dragons and The Strokes will be in the headlines by the first edition of Lollapalooza in January of 2023 along with other numerous international artists including Jackson Wang of K-pop group GOT7.

Lollapalooza India Tickets

The tickets can be availed at Book My Show at a price of 8999 each. Book My Show has mentioned that they will home deliver tickets before the event to all the buyers. This home delivery option is available for Indian buyers only so if you are not residing in it then you are supposed to collect Lollapalooza tickets from box office before the event. Tickets can even be availed at lollaindia.com.

Lollapalooza India LineUp And Performers

Jackson depicted his excitement on his social media handle as he mentioned how excited he is for coming to India as he promised. Other international artists who would make their debut appearances in India include the rock bands Greta Van Fleet and The Wombats; pop artists Alec Benjamin, Chelsea Cutler, the dream-pop group Cigarettes After Sex, Japanese Breakfast and Raveena, and electronic music producers Apashe, Madeon and Zhu, Diplo, Imanbek, Kasablanca.

Despite these artists like AP Dhillon, Prateek Kuhad, Divine, The Yellow Diary, Bloodywood are also part of the lineup in the show. A total of 40 acts are expected to be performed to an audience of nearly 60,000 in Mumbai.
