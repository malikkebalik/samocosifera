Where to Watch No Hard Feelings Online? Exploring All VOD Options

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  459 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

‘No Hard Feelings’ is a romantic comedy movie that stars Jennifer Lawrence as a down-on-her-luck young woman who takes the job of helping a teenager become a man to get him ready to go to college and face the real world. Co-written and directed by Gene Stupnitsky, the film features impressive performances from talented actors, including Andrew Barth Feldman, Laura Benanti, Natalie Morales, and Matthew Broderick. Upon its premiere, the raunchy comedy film opened to mixed reviews from critics, but Lawrence’s ideal comedic timing and dramatic chops keep the viewers hooked from the beginning to the end.

What is No Hard Feelings About?

The narrative focuses on Maddie Barker, a young woman on the verge of bankruptcy and, resultantly, losing her home, who comes across an unusual yet lucrative job listing that can turn things around in her life. All she has to do is date an introverted 19-year-old boy named Percy and ensure he comes out of his shell before leaving for college. But as it turns out, the job is much more complicated than it sounds. Now that your interest has peaked, here are all the ways you can watch the rom-com yourself!

Is No Hard Feelings on Netflix?

No, Netflix doesn’t house ‘No Hard Feelings’ in its expansive catalog. But thanks to its massive collection of movies and TV shows, subscribers have plenty of other options along the same lines, such as ‘Oh, Ramona!‘ and ‘Who’s A Good Boy?.’

Is No Hard Feelings on HBO Max?

HBO Max subscribers might be disappointed that ‘No Hard Feelings’ is not included in the streamer’s library. However, you can turn to rib-tickling alternatives, including ‘Spring Breakers.’

Is No Hard Feelings on Hulu?

Alas! ‘No Hard Feelings’ is unavailable for streaming on Hulu. But the streamer makes up for it by providing similar movies on the platform. We recommend you watch ‘The Girl Next Door‘ and ‘Sharp Stick.’

Is No Hard Feelings on Amazon Prime?

Although ‘No Hard Feelings’ is not a part of Amazon Prime’s regular offering, you have the option of purchasing the Jennifer Lawrence starrer on the streaming giant. To get more information about the same, you can head over here! Also, you can turn to fun alternatives using your subscription. You will likely enjoy watching similar movies like ‘Bad Teacher‘ and ‘Sex and the Teenage Mind.’

Where to Watch No Hard Feelings Online?

‘No Hard Feelings’ has been released in theaters as well as in several VOD platforms, including Vudu, iTunes, AMC on Demand, Spectrum on Demand, Google Play, Microsoft Store, and YouTube. But if you prefer to catch the romance unfold on the big screen, you are more than welcome to check show timings and book tickets on the movie’s official website and Fandango.
