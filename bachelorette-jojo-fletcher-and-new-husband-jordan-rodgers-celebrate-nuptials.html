Bachelorette JoJo Fletcher and new husband Jordan Rodgers celebrate nuptials

EXCLUSIVE: JoJo Fletcher and Jordan Rodgers are married! Ex-Bachelorette stars tie the knot after six year courtship in idyllic ceremony at California winery

By Alesia Stanford For Dailymail.Com

Published: 17:59 EDT, 15 May 2022 | Updated: 18:35 EDT, 15 May 2022

JoJo Fletcher and Jordan Rodgers walked down the aisle Saturday in a beautiful destination wedding in California, sharing their special day with family and friends.

On Friday, JoJo, 31 and Jordan, 33 looked joyous after practicing their vows at the Sunstone Winery in Santa Ynez, California. 

The bride-to-be wore a white fringed sleeveless mini-dress with bows on the straps and paired it with neutral high heels. Her hair was styled in a loose half pony tail.

Cheers: JoJo Fletcher, 31, and Jordan Rodgers, 33, looked joyous after practicing their vows at the Sunstone Winery in Santa Ynez, California Friday

The groom-to-be looked dashing in a white shirt and black suit with a coordinated pocket square for the wedding rehearsal and dinner.

The happy pair met in 2016 when JoJo, a real estate developer, was looking for love as The Bachelorette. 

She and the good-looking sportscaster had instant chemistry and got engaged on the season finale, but waited a while before tying the knot. 

Casual wear:  The guest chose casual wear for the rehearsal and saved the formal clothes for the big day

Location: The rehearsal and wedding took place in the Santa Ynez valley, not far from Santa Barbara, CA

Romantic destination: For their big day, JoJo and Jordan chose the Sunstone Winery in Santa Ynez, California where guests were wined and dined on during the elaborate wedding weekend

The pandemic put a crimp in some of their plans, making it necessary to reschedule their efforts to become husband and wife in 2020 and 2021. 

The couple were finally able to tie the knot in front of family and friends with the bride wearing an exquisite Ines de Santo gown and the groom wearing a classic black tuxedo.

After the ceremony, an elated JoJo changed into a dazzling reception dress with a crisscross halter top, a thigh high slit and fringe. 

Reception dress: After the ceremony, an elated JoJo changed into a dazzling reception dress with a crisscross halter top, a thigh high slit and fringe while Jordan donned a white dinner jacket

Ready to party: The couple, who became engaged after starring on The Bachelorette in 2016 had to delay wedding plans twice due to the pandemic

She styled her long sun-streaked locks in loose curls and wore large diamond studded earrings and a diamond bracelet.

Jordan switched into a white dinner jacket for the reception, so as not to blend in with the groomsmen. 

The blissful newlyweds were spotted sharing a kiss over an elegant Italian dinner of pasta, steak and fish. Bachelor Nation alums Becca Tilley, Raven Gates, and Adam Gottschalk attended the romantic weekend. 

Wedded bliss: The newlyweds were spotted sharing a kiss as they dined with family and friends during their sit-down dinner
