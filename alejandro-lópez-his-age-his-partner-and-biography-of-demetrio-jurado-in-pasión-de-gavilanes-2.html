ALEJANDRO LPEZ: HIS AGE, HIS PARTNER AND BIOGRAPHY OF DEMETRIO JURADO IN PASIN DE GAVILANES 2

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  456 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

If it is about series that became a  true mass phenomenon that crossed borders  ,  ‘Pasión de Gavilanes’ takes the gold prize. A fiction that came to the small screen at the beginning of the 2000s and that conquered viewers with the lives of the Reyes brothers and the Elizondo sisters. A production that has officially returned to our hearts again with ‘Pasión de Gavilanes 2’ and it does so at the hands of new stars in its cast, including Alejandro López, who gives life to Demetrio Jurado.

As we have been introduced, Demetrio Jurado is Gabriela Elizondo’s new best friend. A greedy, evil man, capable of doing anything to fulfill his objective, the latter being Sara Elizondo and her daughter Gaby. For this reason, we will analyze the artist’s professional career and personal life to get to know him a little more and thus be prepared for the new villain of the season. You’ll be surprised!

YOUR PROFESSIONAL CAREER

Born on July 24, 1967 in Bogotá, Colombia, Alejandro López is an internationally recognized television actor . His great debut in the world of acting was in the series ‘N.N’ in 1991 and also in ‘Padres e hijos’, where he gave life to the character of Camilo. A first contact that led him to gain great recognition among the public and among the producers of great fiction.

Subsequently, the actor participated in ‘The mother’, ‘Corazón prohibido’, ‘Amor en forma’, ‘Héroes de shift’, ‘The prosecutor’ and ‘The war of the roses’, among many others. In 2008 he was part of ‘Without breasts there is no paradise’, where he worked with the actor Juan Alfonso Baptista and Carmen Villalobos . Likewise, among the most outstanding series of his career, it is worth mentioning ‘Los herederos del Monte’, ‘La viuda negra’ and ‘El señor de los cielos’.

Recently, the Colombian has joined two new projects for Telemundo, ‘La suerte de Loli’ , where he gave life to the character of Vicente Varela, and ‘Pasión de Gavilanes 2’ . On the other hand, he has also made the leap to the big screen with the movie ‘El capo: el escape del siglo’ in 2016.

HIS PERSONAL LIFE

On a personal level, Alejandro López has also declared himself a faithful follower of social networks. And it is that, currently, he accumulates more than 801,000 followers, only with his Instagram account, a medium where he shows the closest, comical and intimate side of him. In addition, he has also been the image of numerous advertising campaigns and magazine covers, where he brings out the model that he carries within him.

Among his passions, Alejandro López has shown that he is a faithful follower of sports and travel, details that he usually shares on his social networks. On the other hand, in the field of love, the actor maintains a sentimental relationship with the actress Farah Abud, with whom he shares tender moments on social networks.
