Jaden Arroyo Obituary, Death – After a valiant fight against cancer, Jaden Arroyo, a junior at St. Laurence, passed away. We are devastated to inform you of his passing. On Thursday afternoon, he went away without any discomfort, and he was surrounded by his family.

In addition to being a well-liked part of the St. Laurence community, Jaden left an indelible mark on the lives of a great number of people through his powerful, resilient, and upbeat personality. In spite of the challenges he faced, he displayed an unyielding tenacity that served as an example to everyone who had the honored opportunity to meet him.

Over the next several days, we will disseminate information concerning the services and other arrangements that will be performed in order to pay our respects and honor Jaden’s life. A prayer service is scheduled to take place this evening (Friday, December 29th) at seven o’clock at night at St. Richard Church, which is located in Chicago at 5030 S. Kostner Avenue.

It would be greatly appreciated if you would keep Jaden’s family in your thoughts and prayers as they go through this extremely challenging time. Let us come together as a community to show our support for one another and to pay tribute to the memories of Jaden Arroyo, our cherished teammate and fellow Viking.
