The Bryce Young era is over.

Starting Saturday, Sept. 2, Nick Saban’s Alabama Crimson Tide will kick off their first season without their Heisman Trophy winner and attempt to recapture the magic from his three-year stint with the team.

Currently ranked fourth in the country by AP and with returning stars like running back Jase McClellan, cornerback Kool-Aid McKinstry and offensive tackle JC Latham, there is plenty of reason to hope that the squad will return to their winning ways.

However, their quarterback situation is a potential problem area. Coach Saban has three options — Tyler Buchner, Jalen Milroe and Ty Simpson are the contenders — and none have emerged as the clear cut starter leading up to game day.

Therefore, the team will need all the help they can get from the fans at Bryant-Denny Stadium in Tuscaloosa.

That’s where you come in.

Tickets are available for all seven huge home games this year.

And if you want to yell “ROLL TIDE!” at the top of your lungs with your fellow ‘Bama faithful, you can for cheap.

At the time of publication, our team found some tickets going for as low as $5 before fees on Vivid Seats.

Yes, you read that right. Some tickets really are that inexpensive.
