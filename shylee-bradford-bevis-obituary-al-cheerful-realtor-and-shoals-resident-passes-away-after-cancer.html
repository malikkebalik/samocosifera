Shylee Bradford Bevis obituary: AL cheerful Realtor and Shoals resident passes away after cancer

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  554 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Florence, Alabama – The communities of Florence, Alabama, and Sheffield, Alabama, are united in grief as they bid farewell to Shylee Bradford Bevis, a cherished member of their community and a beloved graduate of the University of North Alabama and Bradshaw High School. Shylee Bradford Bevis passed away after a prolonged battle with a devastating cancer, leaving behind a grieving family who remember her as a dedicated mother, wife, and sister.

According to her family, Shylee Bradford Bevis succumbed to cancer complications, bringing her courageous battle to an end. Her passing, which occurred last night, has left a void in the hearts of those who knew and loved her.

Shylee Bradford Bevis was an experienced realtor with an impressive career spanning over 18 years in the industry. She was known for her dedication and expertise, serving as a Buyer’s agent, Listing agent, Relocation specialist, and Short-Sale expert. When asked why she chose a career in real estate, Shylee would often respond, “I Love What I Do & You Will Too!!!” She took pride in her status as a life-long resident of the Shoals, where she had deep roots in the community.

Throughout the past two decades, Shylee Bradford Bevis assisted countless home buyers and sellers in making informed decisions. She was not only a successful realtor but also a dedicated mother to two children and a loving wife to Jeff Bevis. Her commitment to her family was unwavering.

Beyond her professional life, Shylee enjoyed spending quality time with her loved ones. She actively participated in volunteer work at her children’s school and contributed to community events. She often expressed her love for the Shoals, citing the beauty of the Tennessee River, local music, art, and exceptional dining options as some of the many reasons she cherished her hometown.

Zabrina Bruce expressed her  love on the death of Shylee Bradford:

I rarely post on My FB when someone dies …. because in my job I have mostly become numb Death… However there are a few that hit Me in the feels… Shylee Bradford-Bevis is one of those …. She was always Kind and she had a infectious Smile that lit up Every Room! Man….she could sell the crap out of Houses she sold My house in 2 hours She was/is a Real Estate legend (I never not suggested her to everyone in the realestate market) She loved her family and her kids were the light of her life….I am going to miss our random phone calls and texts …. she could keep up with my Sarcasm and was just a great all around human ….the World will Miss you ……

In the wake of her passing, Angela, a fellow Alabama area Realtor, paid tribute to Shylee Bradford Bevis, saying, “The Shoals Area mourns the loss of an extraordinary individual! Shylee Bradford-Bevis, not only an exceptional real estate agent but also a beloved friend to all, has left an indelible mark on our hearts. Her impact on this world is undeniable, and her absence leaves a void that will be deeply felt.”

Shylee Bradford Bevis will be remembered not only for her professional accomplishments but also for the radiant personality and unwavering dedication that touched the lives of those around her. Her legacy in the community will endure as a testament to her enduring spirit.
