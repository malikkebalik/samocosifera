Who is Bizzy Bone?

Bizzy Bone is an American rapper who is widely known for his work as a rapper and one of the members of the Grammy Award-winning group ‘Bone Thugs-n-Harmony’.

He is known as ‘Gambino’, ‘Lil Ripsta’, ‘Bizzyn The Kid’, and ‘Midwest Cowboy’ and is currently working as a solo artist.

Who are the parents of Bizzy Bone?

Born to American parents in Columbus, Ohio, United States of America, during the year of 1976, Bizzy Bone had a rough childhood because of his step-father’s criminal behavior.

When he was four years old, his stepfather McCane II abducted him and his sisters, Hope and Heather telling them that their mother Roseanne Jefferson, and grandmother were dead and they were held captive in Kaw Indian Reservation in Kaw, Oklahoma.

Later they were helped by a babysitter leading to the sibling’s return to the family. He faced molestation during his childhood. He was put in a foster home.

At the age of thirteen, Bizzy moved to his sister’s place near Cleveland, Ohio where he met his former bandmates Layzie Bone, Krayzie Bone, Wish Bone, and Flesh-n-Bone in 1992 in high school.

Talking about the educational background of Bizzy Bone, he was schooled in Cleveland.

Bizzy Bone: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Being a founder of the group ‘Bone Enterprise’ in 1992, he took this group to a certain height and later released his solo albums ‘Heaven’s Movie’ (1998), ‘The Gift’ (2001), ‘Double R’ (2002), ‘Alpha & Omega’ (2004), ‘Speaking in Tongues’ (2005), ‘Thugz Revenge’ (2006) and ‘Story’ (March 21, 2006)  among which ‘Heaven’s Movie’ was one of his hits.

After releasing ‘The Gift’ in 2001 which peaked up at #2 on the US Billboard Independent Albums chart, he released his albums ‘Alpha and Omega’ and  ‘The Beginning and the End’ in 2004.

He also has his own label ‘7th sign’ and is immensely popular for his high-pitched fast-rapping style. Now, his net worth is said to be 500k.

Is Bizzy Bone involved in any rumors and controversies?

Bizzy Bone is often rumored to be a drug addict. During 2012-2013, the rumor that Bizzy is a Drug Abuser gained some highlights.

His weight loss in 2012 also sparked some concerns. He clarified that the weight loss is not due to drugs. In 2001, he and his group were sued by Ruthless Records for damages.
