Kim Hyo-yeon is a South Korean singer, DJ, and television personality best known for being a member of the South Korean girl group Girls’ Generation. Apart from the group, she has provided her vocals to various solo singles such as Mystery, Wannabe, Punk Right Now, Up & Down, and many more. She has a strong social media fan base with more than 5 million followers on Instagram.

    Kim Hyo-yeon Facts

  • Hyoyeon started taking formal dance lessons when she was in elementary school and learned hip-hop, jazz, and Latin dance at a small dance school located in her neighborhood.
  • In 1999, she joined Winners Dance School, South Korea’s one of the most popular dance schools and met Miss A’s Min and the two formed a duo called Little Winners and performed dance routines at various places.
  • In 2000, her mother brought her to the SM Entertainment auditions just because she wanted to meet the South Korean boy band H.O.T.
  • She has learned dance from top choreographers such as Electric Boogaloos, Kim Hye-rang, Poppin Seen, Kwang Hoo (aka Crazy Monkey), Poppin DS, Kwon Seok-jin (aka Locking Khan) and Black Beat’s Shim Jae-won.
  • In 2004, she went to Bejing to study Chinese.
  • Before even making her official debut, she had already worked with famous artists such as Janet Jackson and BoA.
  • In August 2007, she made her official debut with Girls’ Generation.
  • In July 2015, she became 1st of the group to publish her first book in which she shares various tips about beauty, fashion, and lifestyle.
  • In December 2016, she released her debut solo single titled Mystery.
  • In June 2017, Hyoyeon released a new song titled Wannabe.
  • In August 2018, Hyoyeon was revealed as a member of Girls’ Generation’s second sub-unit, Oh!GG.
  • In August 2021, she teamed up with BIBI for a new single titled Second, which debuted at No. 17 position on the Billboard World Digital Songs chart.
  • In May 2022, she released her first EP titled Deep.
  • In August 2023, Hyoyeon released the music video for her new single titled Picture.
