Who is Lea DeLaria?

Lea DeLaria is an American actress and popular Jazz singer. Lea DeLaria as a comedian had invented the famous U-Haul Joke.

Further, the public also recognizes her for her roles in Orange Is the New Black (2013), Cars 3 (2017), and The Oblongs (2001). She will be seen in two movies titled Glitter & Doom and Boston Holy Irresistible that are under the post-production stage.

Lea won Screen Actors Guild Award in 2014, 2015, and 2016 plus was nominated for the same award in 2017.

Who are the parents of Lea DeLaria?

Her birthdate is 23 May 1953 and she was born in Belleville, Illinois, the United States. The name of her mother was Jerry Jean DeLaria and her father was Robert George DeLaria.

Her mom was a homemaker while her dad worked as a Jazz Pianist. Lea’s ancestry is English-Scottish and has two sisters named Thana plus Mary Elizabeth Feltman. Also, she has two brothers Bradley DeLaria and Richard DeLaria.

As per her academics, Lea DeLaria passed out from St. Mary’s Elementary School.

Lea DeLaria also hosted podcasts titled Theater People, The Naked American Songbook, Ways to Change the World, Women’s Hour, The Horrors of Dolores Roach, and more.
