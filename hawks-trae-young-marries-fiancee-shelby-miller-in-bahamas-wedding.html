Hawks' Trae Young marries fiancee Shelby Miller in Bahamas wedding

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Martina Birk

NBA star Trae Young is off the market.

The Atlanta Hawks star officially tied the knot with fiancee Shelby Miller on Saturday as video of the nuptials surfaced on social media.

The couple, both dressed in white, can be seen in the video enjoying their first dance as husband and wife with “Forever Young” written on the dance floor at the Ocean Club in the Bahamas.

Young even got a nice spin and dip of his wife in as their guests looked on.

Young also posted a video to his Instagram Stories of the couple walking on the beach together and their hotel room, which featured Mr. and Mrs. pillows and plenty of rose petals.   

The couple has been engaged since December 2021 and their relationship was made Instagram official back in October 2017 when Young showed up to watch Miller, 27, cheer at an Oklahoma football game. 

They share a 1-year-old son, Tydus together and have a baby girl on the way – which they announced early this month.

Miller posted a photo of Tydus dressing in an open white shirt and tan pants for the wedding.   

Young, 24, is heading into his sixth NBA season after being drafted fifth overall out of Oklahoma in 2018.

He averaged 26.2 points, 10.2 assists and 3.0 rebounds per game last season as the Hawks finish 41-41, had three head coaches and lost in the first round of the playoffs 4-2 to Boston Celtics. 

Young, who has become a Madison Square Garden villain after beating the Knicks in the playoffs in 2021,  signed a five-year, $215 million contract with Atlanta in 2022.
