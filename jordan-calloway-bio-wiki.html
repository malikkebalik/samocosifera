Who is Jordan Calloway?

Jordan Calloway is a 3X Young Artist Award-nominated actor and producer. He is renowned as Khalil Payne of the series, Black Lightning.

Last, in 2019, he appeared in the horror thriller, Countdown as Matt Monroe.

Who are the parents of Jordan Calloway?

Jordan Calloway was born on the 18th of October 1990 in Los Angeles, California in a film background family.

His father, Joseph Calloway is a cinematographer by profession. Whereas, his mother, Tony Calloway is an actress. Currently, Joseph is the director of photography at RoCart, Inc. His parents are no longer together and are separated via divorce.

Jordan Calloway has four siblings including his elder brother, Niles Calloway. Niles is also an actor. They are of British-French ethnicity.

Talking about his educational background, in 2009, she completed High School education at the Maranatha High School. During High Schooldays, he used to play baseball.

Later, Jordan Calloway joined Azusa Pacific University. From there, he earned a bachelor’s degree in production.

Jordan Calloway- Professional Career

Back in 2004, Jordan Calloway landed a prominent role in the TV series, Unfabulous. In the series, he portrayed the character of Zach Carter-Schwartz. For the series, he received three nominations for Young Artist Award in 2005, 2006, and 2008.

Before that, he made his appearance in the numerous including The Parkers, The District, and George Lopez in some minor roles. He first appeared on the TV with the series, The Parkers at the age of 10.

Moving forwards, in 2013, he debuted in movies with the movie, Life of a King. After that, he made his appearance in the TV movies like Studio City and Untitled Paranormal Project. 

Last, in 2018, he appeared in the CW’s superhero drama, Black Lightning. In the series, he played the character of High School student, Khalil Payne.

Besides being an actor, Jordan Calloway is also involved in the production works too. In 2012, he produced his first short movie, Jeffrey’s Fortune.
