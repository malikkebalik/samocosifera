Who is Randy Newman?

Randy Newman is an American Singer-Songwriter, Pianist, Actor, Writer, and Composer.

Randy is best known for the Music Department of Toy Story (Animation Adventure) in 1995, The Princess and the Frog (Animation Comedy) in 2009, and Toy Story 3 (Animation Adventure) in 2010.

Apart from that, Newman is the composer of Monsters at Work (TV Series) in 2021.

Randy Newman: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

Randy Newman was born on 28th November 1943 in Los Angeles, California, U.S. His born name is Randall Stuart Newman.

He is the son of Adele Dixie and Irving George Newman. By profession, his father was an Internist meanwhile his mother was a Secretary.

On the other hand, Randy Newman holds American nationality and belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity.

Moving toward his educational qualifications, he attended University High School. Later, he graduated from UCLA.

Randy Newman: Career

Randy Newman has been a professional songwriter since the age of 17. In 1968, he made his breakthrough with the release of Randy Newman, his first studio album.

In 1970, he released 12 Songs, his second studio album. He issued Sail Away, his third studio album, in 1972. In 1974, he issued his fourth studio album, Good Old Guys. In addition, he issued Little Criminals, his sixth studio album, in 1977.

Moreover, Randy Newman released Land of Dreams, his seventh studio album, in 1988. In 1995, Randy Newman’s Faust, his eighth studio album, was released.

The Randy Newman Songbook Vol.1, his eleventh studio album, was released in 2003. In 2017, he released Dark Matter, the fifth of his Eighteen studio albums.

Randy Newman: Awards and Nominations

  • He is the winner of the Oscar Academy, Awards for Toy Story 3 in 2011.
  • He is the winner of the ASCAP Award for Monsters University in 2014.
  • He is the winner of the Primetime Emmy Awards for Monk in 2010.
  • He is the nominee for CIC Award for Marriage Story in 2020.
