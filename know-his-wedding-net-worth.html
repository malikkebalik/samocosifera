Paco's Wife Was Involved In Controversy For 'In The Heights'

Xavier Zazueta is a man who doesn't engage in controversy. He keeps most of his things to himself and stays away from anything that brings negative energy. Even his wife Melissa is a sweet woman who tries to avoid all of it. But, she did unintentionally find her name being dragged into a controversy. 

The controversy was about the film In the Heights. The movie was an adaptation of Lin-Manuel Miranda's popular broadway show. The movie tried to bring up the social issues of racial inequality, but unfortunately for the makers, it ended up creating the same problem it was trying to fix. 

People called out the movie for the issue of colorism. The story of In the Heights revolved around Black Latinx people, and the film itself lacked in the cast of it. Vox's Aja Romano wrote an article where Miranda apologized to the people for hurting their sentiments through Twitter. He tweeted:

I can hear the hurt and frustration over colorism, of feeling unseen in the feedback. In trying to paint a mosaic of this community, we fell short. I’m truly sorry ... I promise to do better in my future projects, and I’m dedicated to the learning and evolving we all have to do to make sure we are honoring our diverse and vibrant community.
